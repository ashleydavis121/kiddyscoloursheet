Printing Tips 🖨️

Get the best results when printing your colouring sheets!!

📄

Use Plain White A4 Paper

For best results always use plain white A4 paper. Thicker paper 80gsm or above works even better for colouring!!

🖨️

Print In High Quality

Set your printer to high quality or best quality mode for the sharpest and clearest lines!!

⬛

Print In Black And White

Always print in black and white to save ink and get the clearest colouring lines for kids!!

📐

Fit To Page

Select fit to page in your print settings to make sure the colouring sheet fills the whole page perfectly!!

🎨

Best Colouring Tools

Felt tip pens, coloured pencils and crayons all work brilliantly!! Felt tips give the brightest colours!!

♾️

Print As Many Times As You Like

You can print each sheet as many times as you want!! Perfect for classrooms and families with multiple children!!
