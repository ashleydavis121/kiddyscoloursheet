⬇️ Downloads

How do I download my colouring sheet??

+
After completing your payment through PayPal you will receive a download link instantly!! Simply click the link to download your colouring sheet as a PDF file directly to your device!!

What format is the file??

+
All our colouring sheets are delivered as high quality PDF files!! PDF files are easy to open on any device — computer, tablet, or phone — and print perfectly every time!!

Do I need special software to open it??

+
No special software needed!! Most devices can open PDF files automatically. If you need a PDF viewer we recommend Adobe Acrobat Reader which is completely free to download from adobe.com!!

🖨️ Printing

How many times can I print it??

+
You can print your colouring sheet as many times as you like!! Once you purchase and download it you own it forever!! Perfect for families with multiple children or teachers who need lots of copies!!

What size paper should I use??

+
We recommend printing on plain white A4 paper for the best results!! For even better quality try using 80gsm or thicker paper which holds colour beautifully. Always print in black and white to save ink!!

Can I use these in my classroom??

+
Absolutely!! Our colouring sheets are perfect for classroom use!! You can print as many copies as you need for your students. Our sheets are designed to be fun, educational and engaging for children of all ages!!
